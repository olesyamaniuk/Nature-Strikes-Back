About the Game

Nature Strikes Back is an exciting puzzle game that invites players to help nature reclaim its dominance over a world overtaken by industrialization. Through strategic matching and problem-solving, players unleash the power of natural elements — forests, rivers, and wildlife — to restore the balance between man and nature. With captivating levels, beautiful graphics, and a message of environmental conservation, Nature Strikes Back offers an engaging and eco-friendly gaming experience.
